Black Keys Tribute

Bio

The Black Keys are an American Rock banned formed in 2001. THe two members are Dan Auerback on the guitar and vocals, and Patrick Carney on the Drums. The band is famous for their garage rock style and began as an independent act. Recording in their basement and self-producing their records they quickly became a hit. Their style is a blues rock sound mixed with raw rock. Their hit single "tighten up" won three Grammy Awards and their hit album El Camino peaked number two on the Billboard 200 chart.
